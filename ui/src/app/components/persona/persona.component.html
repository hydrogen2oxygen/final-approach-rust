Choose a Persona

Press F-2 to F-5 keys for switching between different personas.

F2 - DESIGNER

Designs the map and assigns them to territories.

F3 - MANAGER

Manage the territories, assigning them to preachers or groups.

F4 - PREACHER

It simulates the view from the perspective of a preacher.

F5 - GROUP LEADER

It simulates the view from the perspective of a group leader.
